## Purpose

This document is the operational index of SquadVault's binding artifacts and their
tier of authority.

It is a **registry, not doctrine** (per Reset Memo v1.0 section 6.4). The Map records
which documents bind, at what tier, and under what conditions; it does not itself
constitute binding doctrine. Authority resides in the Canonical Operating Constitution
and the subordinate documents it governs.

---

## Registration as Commissioning

Per Reset Memo v1.0 section 6.2, a SquadVault document acquires binding status by being
registered in this Map. Authoring a document does not commission it; registration does.
A document referenced in code, in another document, or in conversation but not registered
here is provisional and must not be treated as authoritative.

The registration-as-commissioning mechanism was established in v1.6. This Map (v1.7)
operates under the established mechanism without bootstrap-style reflexivity; it
self-registers as an ordinary Tier 1 entry (see Tier 1 below).

### Curative Ratification (One-Time, Concluded)

Per Reset Memo v1.0 section 6.5, the ten documents enumerated in section 5 of that memo
(the "Finding-3 set") were granted binding status by the memo itself, effective at commit
`bb0f325`. This was a one-time curative amnesty, now concluded. Documents authored after
`bb0f325` are subject to registration-as-commissioning without exception: provisional
until registered.
