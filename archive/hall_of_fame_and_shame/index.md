# PFL Buddies — Hall of Fame & Shame

The Digital Archive: PFL Buddies, 2010 – 2025. The league has been running continuously since 1983; the archive begins where the digital records begin.

This archive surfaces three views of the digital era:

- [Championship Roll](./championship_roll.md) — per-season league champions; aggregate title counts.
- [Worst-Season Tracking](./worst_seasons.md) — the league's worst-record seasons, top-N.
- [Blowouts Hall](./blowouts_hall.md) — the league's largest single-week margins, top-N.

Content is regenerated from canonical league events at the commissioner's election; the regeneration is operationally deterministic, and the git history is the archive's version history.
