Week 1

KP's Playmakers opened the season with the week's high score, putting up 129.6 behind Josh Allen's 37.5-point opener to beat Steve's Warmongers 129.6-118.0. Purple Haze crushed Robb's Raiders 119.6-89.9 in the week's biggest blowout. Pat got 25.3 from Joe Mixon while Robb managed just 89.9 points despite a 27.3-point kicker performance from Jake Moody. Robb left 51.2 points on the bench with Jameson Williams (21.9) watching from the sidelines, but even optimal lineups wouldn't have saved him from this margin.

Ben squeaked past Miller 105.7-101.2 in the week's closest game, decided by 4.6 points. Both teams left significant production on their benches — Ben had Jayden Reed's 31.1 points sitting unused, while Miller benched Isaiah Likely's 21.6. Brandon beat Michele 111.2-98.9, with Brandon picking up Baker Mayfield for $11 on waivers after the fact.

Stu handled Eddie 119.4-92.3 behind Jalen Hurts' 23.2 points. Eddie got 32.2 from Anthony Richardson but couldn't overcome Chris Olave's 2.1-point dud. Eddie moved quickly afterward, trading Olave to Brandon for Sam LaPorta and adding T.J. Hockenson on waivers for free.

The week's waiver activity was headlined by KP's $30 bid for Jaylen Wright and Stu's $16 pickup of Greg Dortch, while several teams made free agent additions to shore up their rosters.
