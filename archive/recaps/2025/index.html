**Week 4: Two Undefeateds, One Miracle**

KP and Pat remain the only unbeaten teams after four weeks, but they took very different paths to 4-0. The Playmakers rolled Eddie 143-117 behind Bo Nix's 33 points, extending their win streak while Eddie dropped his third straight. The #6 seed upset the #3 seed in the highest-scoring game of the week, with Patrick Mahomes dropping 38 for Michele and Omarion Hampton contributing 25 for Ben. Both teams left significant points on their benches, but Ben's lineup held by three-quarters of a point.

Miller extended his win streak to two games with a 109.65-92.15 victory over Steve's Warmongers, while Robb snapped his three-game skid by beating Brandon 119.05-98.60. Brandon remains winless at 0-4, and his FAAB investments continue to underwhelm — Brian Thomas Jr. has averaged just 7.5 points through four starts despite costing $51. Steve's $46 investment in Brock Bowers (8.5 points per start) and Eddie's $32 spend on Ladd McConkey (6.3 points per start) haven't paid off either, with all three players well below the 17.2-point league average for starters.

Robb's $40 pickup of Brock Purdy delivered immediately with 24.75 points in the win, while Michele's waiver work paid off with a $1 Woody Marks pickup that scored 25.90 points on his bench. The FAAB spending has been aggressive early — Robb leads at $50 spent, followed by Brandon at $35 and KP at $30.
