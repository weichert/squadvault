# PFL Buddies - Draft History Vault

The Draft History Vault: PFL Buddies auction era, 2018 - 2025. The 2021 auction is not represented in the digital substrate. Leaderboards cover seven seasons: 2018, 2019, 2020, 2022, 2023, 2024, 2025.

This archive surfaces three views of the auction era:

- [Auction-Most-Expensive History](./most_expensive.md) - the all-time most-expensive auction pick across positions, plus per-position records.
- [Auction-Bust Hall](./bust_hall.md) - the league's worst-performing auction picks across the auction era, ranked by severity.
- [Auction-Bargain Hall](./bargain_hall.md) - the league's best-value auction picks across the auction era, ranked by dollars-per-point.

Content is regenerated from canonical league events at the commissioner's election; the regeneration is operationally deterministic, and the git history is the archive's version history.
