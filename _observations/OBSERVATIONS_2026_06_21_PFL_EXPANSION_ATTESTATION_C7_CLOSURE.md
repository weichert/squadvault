## Note for the record

This is the first instance in the project's attestation saga where a generated value survives
attestation unchanged. It does not weaken the discipline - it confirms it. "Phony Bowl" made the
expansion plausible, but plausibility is the same inference the generator could (and did) make;
the value was a hypothesis until the founder attested it first-hand. The lesson is unchanged:
a guess is not engraved because it is plausible, even when it later proves correct.
